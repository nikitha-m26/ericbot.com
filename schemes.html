Schemes

Women Empowerment

Empowered Women, Empower Women.

Beti Bacho Beti Padho

Beti Bacho Beti Padho is a central government scheme that is benificial across the country for girl child. The primary goal of this scheme is to save the girl child from societal issues like gender-based abortions and advance girl child education across the country.

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana is an unique government-sponsered savings scheme which promotes a girl child, as the predominant account holder whereas the parent/legal guardian as a joint account holder.The account can be openend before 10 years of age of the girl child.

Balika Samriddhi Yojana

The Balika Samriddhi Yojana is a scholarship programme intended to offer financial assistance for below- poverty line young girls and their families. The schemes main goal is to boost their standing in society, improve girl's marriageable age.

One Stop Center Scheme

One Stop Center Scheme addresses Gender-Based Violence. It was implemented from 1st April 2015. The major aim of the scheme is to facilitate acess to wide range of services to women affected by violence.

CBSE Udaan Scheme

CBSE Udaan Scheme for girl child is managed and run by the Central Board of Secondary Education via the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. The aim of this scheme is to raise girl's enrolement across India at prominent engineering anf technical colleges.

Working Womens Hostel

It was implemented in 1972-73 of grant-in-aid for construction of new buildings for providing hostel facilities to working women in cities, smaller towns and also in rural areas where employment opportunities for women exist and for womens better safety.

Scholarship Schemes

Strive for Progress, not Perfection

Pre-University

This scholarship is an award for pre-university students, studying in various sectors like science, commerce, fine arts. NMMS(National Means-Cum-Merit Scholarship), Vidya Siri Scholarship, Jindal Scholarship are some of the scholarships that can be availed by the students.

Degree

This scholarship is an award for university students, studying in various sectors like applied science, commerce, management, business, computer science, medical and pharmacy.L'Oreal, G.P Birla(G. P. Birla Educational Foundation), CLP India are some of the scholarships that can be availed by the students.

Master's Degree

This scholarship is an award for university students,that are willing to pursue higher education in a particular feild like medical, business, md, phd and other higher courses available. AICTE(The All India Council for Technical Education), S.N Bose, GyanDhan are some of the scholarships that can be availed by the students.

National Means-Cum-Merit Scholarship

National Means-Cum-Merit Scholarship(NMMS) is a Centrally sponsored scholarship scheme. It is implemented by the Departmentof School Education & Literacy under the Ministry of Human Resource Development with the objective to financially support the meritorious students of economically weaker sections of the society.

G.P Birla

G. P. Birla Educational Foundation is offering G. P. Birla Scholarship 2020 to meritorious students from West Bengal,who have passed Class 12 examination in 2020. The scholarship can be availed by students pursuing courses in any stream including Science, Commerce, Arts, Engineering, Medicine, Architecture etc.

AICTE

The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) is an apex national-level advisory body for imparting technical education in India. It not only provides accreditation to colleges and institutions involved in technical education but also offers a number of scholarships to students who are willing to pursue a technical degree course.
